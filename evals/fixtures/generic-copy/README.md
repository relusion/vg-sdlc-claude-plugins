# Acme Sync — Project Overview

In today's fast-paced digital landscape, Acme Sync empowers organizations to
seamlessly leverage their data and unlock new levels of operational efficiency.
Our robust, scalable, and game-changing platform is a transformative solution
that drives sustainable growth across the enterprise.

## What it does

Acme Sync replicates records between PostgreSQL databases across regions. It
authenticates every request with OAuth2 and targets 99.9% uptime under the v2.3
service contract. Replication lag stays under 500ms at the p95.

## Getting started

Simply configure your connection string and Acme Sync will effortlessly handle
the rest, delivering a seamless and frictionless experience for your entire
team from day one.
