material identified in [THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md](./THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md)
remains subject to its stated terms. Apache-2.0 permits use, modification,

subject to its terms.

The license grants for a version already distributed under Apache-2.0 continue
according to the license. A later change in project policy cannot withdraw
those grants from an already-published version.

The project's current policy is to keep original project material accepted and
published in this repository under Apache-2.0 and not move existing repository
content behind a paywall. This policy describes how the project is maintained;
it is not an additional license term or a promise about unpublished future
work.

Possible examples include hosted or managed services, organization-wide policy
management, retained reporting, enterprise administration, and support. Any
such offering would be separate from this repository and would not change the
license that applies to material published here.

Apache-2.0 also permits others to build and sell products or services using
the material it covers, subject to the license. A separate paid offering would
not create an exclusive right to those use cases.

or a conformity assessment.
